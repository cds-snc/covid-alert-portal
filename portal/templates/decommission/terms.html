The COVID Alert Portal is only for authorized staff in Canada.

By using the portal, you agree to follow your health authority’s procedures.

You also agree to:

Only generate keys for real people

Give only 1 key per positive test.

For a sample key, visit "Give a key".

Confirm the person meets 2 conditions

They've:

  • Tested positive for COVID-19, and,
  • Installed the COVID Alert app on their phone.

Get the person’s consent before texting a key

Some people may want you to text the one-time key to their mobile phone. If your health authority offers this feature, the portal displays the button “Text key to person”.

Prevent others from using your account

Protect your username and password, even from other people working for a health authority.

Keep the portal secure

Immediately report any security issues to your administrator and security@cds-snc.ca

If we detect any misuse of the portal or security issues with your account, we’ll immediately disable your account.
