The COVID Alert Portal is only for authorized healthcare workers in Canada.

By using the portal, you agree to:

  • Follow your healthcare authority’s procedures.
  • Only give one key per each patient diagnosed with COVID-19 by your healthcare authority. Do not generate a key for any other reason.
  • Prevent other people from using your account. You must protect your username and password, even from other people working for a healthcare authority.
  • Immediately report any security issues with your account to both:
    • your administrator
    • security@cds-snc.ca

If we detect any misuse of the portal or security issues with your account, we’ll immediately disable your account.

For questions about these terms of use, or to report a technical issue, please contact us.
